Development Team

UIMP / UNICAN / CSIC - Spain

Students and professors of the Master in Data Science joined efforts to offer a tool to promote democratic change by involving citizens in decisions about local digital strategies. The App makes use of the netBravo open database of the JRC (Joint Research Centre) to determine the closest open WiFi networks.
